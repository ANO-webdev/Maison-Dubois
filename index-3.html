Our Story

Three Generations of Artisan Baking

Founded by Henri Dubois in 1962 in the heart of Saint-Germain-des-Prés, Maison Dubois has been a neighbourhood institution for over sixty years. What began as a small corner boulangerie has grown into one of Paris's most beloved addresses for bread, pastry, and fine coffee.

Today, Henri's granddaughter Claire continues the tradition — sourcing organic stone-milled flour from a farm in the Loire Valley and slow-fermenting every sourdough for a minimum of 18 hours. Nothing here is rushed. Everything here is made with love.
